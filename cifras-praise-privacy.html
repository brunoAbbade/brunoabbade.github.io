🔒 Privacy Policy — Renascer Praise Cifras

Last updated: June 16, 2026

👋 Introduction

Renascer Praise Cifras is a gospel music chords and lyrics app developed by Bruno Abbade. This Privacy Policy describes how the application handles user information.

📦 Data collected

Renascer Praise Cifras does not collect, transmit or share any personal data with third parties.

All information generated by the app is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it. The only data saved locally is:

  • 🎵 Songs saved as favorites by the user
  • ⚙️ User preferences (light/dark theme, default key, font size)
  • 📖 Recently viewed songs history

🚫 What we don't use

  • 📊 Analytics or tracking services
  • 📢 Advertising
  • 🔐 Social media login or account registration
  • 🌐 Connection to external servers for data collection
  • 📷 Camera, microphone, location or contacts

🔑 Permissions

Renascer Praise Cifras does not request any special device permissions.

👶 Children and Families Policy

Renascer Praise Cifras is safe for all ages and complies with the Google Play Families Policy. The app does not collect personal data, does not display advertising, has no social features, and does not transmit device identifiers.

⚖️ Legal Compliance

This policy complies with COPPA (USA), GDPR (European Union) and LGPD (Brazil).

📝 Changes to this policy

Any updates will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
