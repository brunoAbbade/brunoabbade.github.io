Chord Book

Your chord chart repertoire, organized.

📖 About

Chord Book is a chord chart organizer developed by Bruno Abbade. Keep every song you play in one place — organized into albums, with chords, tabs and lyrics ready to read on stage or in practice.

✨ Features

  • 🎵 Organize chord charts into albums and favorites
  • 🎸 Transpose the key of any song instantly
  • 🎹 Chord diagrams for guitar, ukulele and piano
  • 📂 Import PDF or text chord charts
  • 🌓 Dark mode reading view, built for the stage
  • ☁️ Optional backup to your own Google Drive
